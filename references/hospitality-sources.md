# Hospitality Sources

Curated catalog of trade publications, news sites, and research outlets used by the `firecrawl-plugin` skills for market scans, competitive intelligence, and industry context. Grouped by publication type. Every URL is verified as of 2026-04-20.

---

## Trade press (hospitality-wide)

### Skift

- **URL:** https://skift.com/
- **News section URL:** https://skift.com/restaurants/ (restaurants vertical); general travel/hospitality: https://skift.com/
- **Focus:** Large-format, analytical coverage of travel, hospitality, and F&B business strategy. Known for the Skift Research arm and conference circuit.
- **Best for:** M&A news, macro industry trends, hospitality brand strategy, hotel + restaurant intersection stories
- **Scrape notes:** Most news articles are free. Skift Research reports are paywalled. No known CAPTCHA on article pages.

### Hotel News Now (now part of CoStar News)

- **URL:** https://www.hotelnewsnow.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.hotelnewsnow.com/ (redirects auto-route to CoStar)
- **Focus:** Hotel operations, performance, development, public hotel companies. Incorporates STR data trends.
- **Best for:** Hotel REIT + public company news, STR performance data references, hotel pipeline/development, executive interviews
- **Scrape notes:** Accessible for free, no paywall. Legacy hotelnewsnow.com URLs auto-redirect to CoStar.

### Hospitality Technology

- **URL:** https://hospitalitytech.com/
- **News section URL:** https://hospitalitytech.com/ (homepage is the news feed)
- **Magazine archive:** https://hospitalitytech.com/magazine
- **Focus:** Technology trends in lodging + foodservice. Publishes annual Restaurant Technology Study and Hotel Technology Study.
- **Best for:** Workforce tech news, POS/PMS announcements, AI adoption benchmarks, IT budget data, Fourth category media coverage
- **Scrape notes:** Articles free. Research reports require registration. Minimal paywall friction.

## Restaurant industry

### Nation Restaurant News (NRN)

- **URL:** https://www.nrn.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.nrn.com/ (homepage + topic feeds)
- **Focus:** Business intelligence for restaurant and foodservice industry. Founded 1967. Publishes the NRN Top 200 and Power List.
- **Best for:** Chain restaurant news, M&A, franchisee activity, operator rankings, leadership changes at major brands
- **Scrape notes:** Most content free, some premium / gated. Full archive requires subscription.

### Restaurant Business Online

- **URL:** https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/article/restaurant-business
- **Focus:** Sister publication to NRN (also Informa). Focus on entrepreneurship, innovation, and growth. Publishes Technomic Top 500.
- **Best for:** Top 500 chain performance data, Sysco and distributor news, financial deep-dives on operators
- **Scrape notes:** Free articles with light registration prompts. RSS feed at https://www.restaurantbusinessonline.com/rss/restaurant-daily

### Restaurant Dive

- **URL:** https://www.restaurantdive.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.restaurantdive.com/ (homepage = news)
- **Technology beat:** https://www.restaurantdive.com/topic/technology/
- **Focus:** Daily-newsletter format covering consumer trends, restaurant tech, policy, operations. Owned by Informa TechTarget.
- **Best for:** Breaking news, policy/regulation developments, restaurant tech launches, IPO and capital markets news
- **Scrape notes:** Free access, no paywall. Clean article structure - Firecrawl-friendly.

### FSR Magazine (Full-Service Restaurants)

- **URL:** https://www.fsrmagazine.com/
- **Labor section:** https://www.fsrmagazine.com/labor-employees
- **Focus:** Full-service restaurant concepts - fine dining, casual, chains, independents. Covers operations, food, beverage, and design.
- **Best for:** Full-service operator news, labor/employee trends (very relevant for Fourth), franchise development, menu/concept stories
- **Scrape notes:** Free. Publishes a print edition; website has the full article archive.

### QSR Magazine

- **URL:** https://www.qsrmagazine.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.qsrmagazine.com/recent
- **Focus:** Quick-service and fast-casual industry. Traditional fast food, fast-casual, coffee/snacks, convenience.
- **Best for:** Franchise news, unit growth announcements, QSR concepts, quick-service labor and tech trends
- **Scrape notes:** Free, article-level access works well with Firecrawl. Note: QSR Media at qsrmedia.com is a separate Australian publication - do not confuse.

## Hotel industry

### Hotel Management

- **URL:** https://www.hotelmanagement.net/
- **News section URL:** https://www.hotelmanagement.net/ (homepage) plus https://www.hotelmanagement.net/tech for tech beat
- **Focus:** Hotel operations, marketing, finance, technology, design. Trade journal published by Questex (founded 1922).
- **Best for:** Hotel operator news, F&B operations in hotels, brand partnerships, hotel technology adoption
- **Scrape notes:** Free access, most articles public. Digital magazine at hotelmanagementdigital.com.

### LODGING Magazine

- **URL:** https://lodgingmagazine.com/
- **News section URL:** https://lodgingmagazine.com/ + https://lodgingmagazine.com/category/industrynews/
- **Focus:** Official publication of American Hotel and Lodging Association (AHLA). Hotel news, development, design, F&B, management.
- **Best for:** AHLA-adjacent industry news, hotel policy/advocacy stories, leadership appointments, AHLA event coverage
- **Scrape notes:** Free access. Reaches 38,000+ hospitality executives. Subscribe for daily digest.

### HOTELS Magazine

- **URL:** https://hotelsmag.com/
- **News archive:** https://hotelsmag.com/news/
- **News-categories hotels:** https://hotelsmag.com/news-categories/hotels-news/
- **Focus:** Global hotel market. Senior corporate + property-level management in 160+ countries. Founded 1966.
- **Best for:** International hotel news, global brand launches, luxury/wellness concepts, international acquisitions
- **Scrape notes:** Free articles, digital magazine issues available online.

## Labor / HR / workforce (cross-industry, hospitality-relevant)

### HR Dive

- **URL:** https://www.hrdive.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.hrdive.com/ (homepage is news feed)
- **HR management topic:** https://www.hrdive.com/topic/hr-management/
- **Focus:** HR news and analysis - talent acquisition, L&D, D&I, compensation, benefits, onboarding. Owned by Informa TechTarget.
- **Best for:** HR regulation changes, labor policy, compliance news, workforce trends that affect hospitality operators
- **Scrape notes:** Free access, clean article structure. Good Firecrawl target.

### SHRM (Society for Human Resource Management)

- **URL:** https://www.shrm.org/
- **News section URL:** https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/news
- **Topics index:** https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/topics
- **Focus:** HR association news covering labor and employee relations, recruiting, total rewards, talent analytics, compliance.
- **Best for:** Authoritative HR policy + regulation coverage, BLS employment data analysis, labor market trends, SHRM research
- **Scrape notes:** Most news articles free. Research reports and some premium content behind SHRM member login.

### Workforce.com (blog + news)

- **URL:** https://workforce.com/
- **News section URL:** https://www.workforce.com/news
- **UK blog:** https://ukblog.workforce.com/
- **Customer stories:** https://workforce.com/customers
- **Focus:** Hourly-workforce management content. Compliance (minimum wage, overtime, breaks), HR/payroll trends, AI labor forecasting.
- **Best for:** Compliance reference (state-by-state wage and labor law), hourly-workforce tech commentary, UK/Europe labor coverage
- **Scrape notes:** Free. Note: Workforce.com is also a product competitor in scheduling/time tracking - useful for market context as well as news.

## Adding a new source

When adding a new publication to this catalog:

1. Visit the homepage and verify: (a) the site is active, (b) news is published regularly (within the last 30 days), (c) articles are accessible without an enterprise paywall.
2. Identify the dedicated news feed URL (often `/news/`, `/latest/`, `/topic/<beat>`) - add it alongside the homepage URL.
3. Note paywall or registration friction explicitly so skills can route around it.
4. Add the source to the appropriate section (trade press, restaurant, hotel, labor) - create a new section if the publication opens up a new beat Fourth cares about (e.g., food delivery, loyalty tech).
5. If the source has a high signal-to-noise ratio for a specific Fourth research task, add a matching example query at the bottom.
